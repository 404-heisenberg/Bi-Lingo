# Bi-Lingo — AI tutoring in your home language

**Bi-Lingo** is an AI tutoring platform that helps South African learners understand CAPS curriculum concepts in any of the 11 official languages. Built for the 77% of learners ed-tech forgot — the ones taught in English but who learn best in the language they speak at home.

> For every child. In every language.



## The problem

The South African Constitution promises education in a child's home language. For three decades, that promise has been broken.

- **81%** of Grade 4 learners can't read for meaning in *any* language.
- **9M+** African-language speakers are stranded in English-only classrooms.

Children don't fail because they can't learn — they fail because they're taught in a language they don't yet understand.

  

## Features

- **AI Tutor** — Ask questions in any SA language. Mrs. Ndlovu (isiZulu) and Auntie Mpho (Sesotho) explain CAPS concepts with culturally relevant examples drawn from the learner's own world.
- **AI Quiz Generator** — Study a topic, then generate a trilingual quiz (English, isiZulu, Sesotho) to test your understanding.
- **Structured Lessons** — CAPS-aligned lessons for Grades 4–12 across Maths, Natural Sciences, English, Life Sciences, and more. Progress syncs so learners pick up where they left off.
- **Teacher Dashboard** — See exactly which learners struggle because of English comprehension, not because they don't know the material. Get actionable intervention suggestions.
- **Voice Input** — Ask questions out loud in your home language. No typing needed for younger learners.



## How it works

1. **Pick a language** — Choose from all 11 official languages. One tap to switch.
2. **Ask in your words** — Type or speak a question the way you'd ask your gogo. No English required.
3. **Learn through local examples** — CAPS concepts re-explained with analogies from your neighbourhood — not a translated textbook.
4. **Grow with every lesson** — Progress syncs with teachers and parents. The whole village sees the learning happen.
